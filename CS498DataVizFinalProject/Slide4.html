Country Impact (2015)

In 2015, the highest concentration of Malaria cases and deaths were reported in Africa, and this was followed by South East Asia. When looking at a Country based break down of Malaria cases, and deaths, we see that 13 countries reported 76% of the Malaria cases World wide and 72% of the Malaria Deaths World wide. Of these countries, in Africa, Nigeria reported 29% of the Word wide cases, and 26% of the World wide Deaths. In South East Asia, India reported 6% of the World wide cases, and 6% of the World wide Deaths.

Use the User Input buttons below to view the 2015 Malaria Cases by Country, or the 2015 Malaria Deaths by Country, in the Visualization.

About the Visualization

This Visualization follows the Interactive Slide show hybrid structure. Slide 4 depicts a Scene that conveys to the user, the impact of Malaria on various Countries of the World. The Visualization in this scene is a donut chart, where each country is represented by a slice. The chart shows the top 13 countries, in terms of Malaria incidences and fatalities. The remainder of the countries are grouped in a slice labeled 'Other'. The area of each slice is proportional to either the number of cases of malaria reported in the Country, or the Number of Deaths caused by Malaria in the Country, depending on the users selection of whether to view a graph of the Cases, or a graph of the Deaths. The Triggers in this Visualization include the Users selection of a button that determines whether they want to view a graph of the Malaria Cases by Country, or a graph of Malaria Deaths by Country. This selection drives a Parameter that transitions between the two datasets, and animates the Visualization to display the graph that the user wishes to see. Additional Triggers in this scene include Mouse Over and Mouse Out events. When a user Mouses over a slice on the graph, it displays a Tool Tip Annotation , that shows the Country selected and the percentage of world wide Malaria cases/deaths attributed to the country that was moused over. It also changes the opacity of the slice that was moused over. Each Slice in the Graph is also Annotated to show the Country that the Slice corresponds to, and its percentage of World Wide Malaria cases/deaths , based on the user's selection of whether to view Cases/Deaths. Additional Parameters in this Interactive Slide show are defined by the Slide Numbers that the User can click on, to advance the presentation. These are provided both in a navigation bar at the top of the page, and as Navigation buttons at the bottom of the page. These Parameters determine which slide is displayed to the user. The user can Trigger a change in the scenes contained within the Slide show, by modifying these Parameters.
